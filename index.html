Our Story

From Unimaginable Loss,
Boundless Love

A family's choice to transform the deepest grief into the most radiant hope.

"A guiding light of hope"

"Our mission is to be a guiding light of hope in the darkness by providing help to the homeless population without discrimination."

— Zach's Rays of Hope Foundation

Zach's life was defined by his love for others — and his legacy carries that forward.

Zach was the son of Danny and Lesa — a young man whose compassion for others knew no bounds. Tragically, Zach's life was cut short far too soon.

In the face of unimaginable grief, Danny and Lesa made a remarkable choice: they decided to honor Zach's memory by becoming a guiding light for others. They founded Zach's Rays of Hope Foundation to provide homeless individuals across Oklahoma City and surrounding areas with backpacks filled with life's essentials — serving everyone with the same open heart Zach always had.

Every backpack delivered is a ray of hope — a tangible expression of the love Zach carried in his heart, now shared with those who need it most.

Leadership

Founders & Board of Directors

Five people united by one belief: that every person deserves warmth, safety, and hope, no matter their circumstances.

Danny Reynolds

Founder & President

Why I'm the CEO of Zach's Rays of HopeThe reason I founded Zach's Rays of Hope was because there is a loss of compassion, dignity, hope, and respect given to the unhoused community, which goes against the principles I have learned throughout my experiences as a dad, follower of Christ, and a member of a charitable fraternal organization. I help the unhoused in memory of our youngest son, Zach, who cared for and helped others in need.

Lesa Reynolds

Co-Founder & Treasurer

Why I helped start Zach's Rays of Hope FoundationI am a retired nurse, and helping others gives my life purpose and joy. I am rewarded as much as the ones we help by seeing the joy in their faces as they receive items to endure the cold. I heard of a young man being denied shelter because of being a part of the LGBTQ community. The young man froze outside of the shelter's doors. I began thinking about my grief and how there was another mom grieving now. I wanted to be a part of something that believed in everyone being treated equally and with respect.

Daniel Reynolds

Board Member

Why I'm with Zach's Rays of HopeI am a business owner that provides fair wages and benefits to my employees. The current economy has made it difficult on many to afford housing. The unhoused deserve opportunities to assist them with services that help them to get back on their feet, and that can help our society as a whole.

Jeremy Sampson

Board Member

Jeremy is the owner of Rinnovo Solutions and Salty Life Tee. Veteran, father of five, and grandfather of two. He is an entrepreneur, humanitarian, and graduate of Houghton University with a Bachelor's in business. He has almost two decades of experience consulting executives and business owners and training new leaders and sales people. He lives through his core tenet and noble purpose: in every encounter to enrich the lives of all involved — spiritually, intellectually, mentally, and physically.

Taylor Bell

Board Member

Why Zach's Rays of HopeI am a dad and small business owner that believes in community. A community is strengthened by all members pulling together to help lift others back up. This is why I am a board member for Zach's Rays of Hope Foundation, so that I can make a difference in the communities we serve.
